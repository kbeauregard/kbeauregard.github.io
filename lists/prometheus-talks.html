The TSDB that sits at the core of the Prometheus monitoring system started with a similar approach and was built on top of LevelDB. A journey from fundamental challenges of TSDB design to details of the Prometheus storage layer. Let me show you the dark arts of inter-process communication, and how we ended up fully supporting metrics in Ruby.
